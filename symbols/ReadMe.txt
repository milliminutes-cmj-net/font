You can make nice stone walls and wood fences with this font by spraying a wood or stone texture into the font characters (while selected) using Paint Shop Pro Picture Tubes or similar texture sprayer.  Finish by inverting selection and adding grout color texture.  Drop shadows can be added for 3D texture effects.

Use your graphics programs perspective tools to make fences 3D.
